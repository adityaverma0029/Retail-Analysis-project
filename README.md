**Retail Marketing Analytics Project**  
This Jupyter notebook provides a complete end-to-end analytics pipeline for retail sales data, focusing on customer segmentation, sales optimization, and actionable business insights.
  
**Project Overview**  
The project analyzes 10,000 retail sales records across 19 columns (6.17 MB), covering customer behavior, product performance, and operational metrics from 2022-2023. It delivers cleaned datasets, 27 visualizations, 7 reports, and executive recommendations for marketing strategies.
  
**Key Features**  
-Data Processing: Handles missing values (0.04%), duplicates (0%), data type optimization (memory reduced to 3.49 MB).  
-EDA Highlights: Electronics leads revenue (30.45%), Q1 peaks at 34.2% of sales, weekend sales 0.9% higher.  
​-Customer Analytics: RFM segmentation (e.g., 353 Champions contribute 28% revenue), 96.3% repeat rate.  
​-Advanced Models: K-Means clustering, cohort analysis, CLV (avg $7,521, 150x CAC).  
  
**Usage**  
-Run notebook sequentially for full pipeline.  
-Import outputs to Power BI/Tableau for dashboards.  
-All checkpoints and reports saved in data/, outputs/reports/, outputs/figures/.
